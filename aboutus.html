About Us

Considering the various security threats happening across every part of Nigeria, “SecuredAlat” is a proprietary app that enables its users to post activities of security threats within a geographical location to flag such location as a hotspot and encourages its users to avoid such areas at certain time.

The development of this software application was initiated and inspired by the experiences of the developers who live in Nigeria. Considering the high level of crimes and security threats reported each day in Nigeria, the developers decided to take up this task to provide a better comprehensive and reliable platform to improve the security and safety of the people (residents and visitors in Nigeria) and their properties.
